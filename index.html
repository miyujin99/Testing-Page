STEP 2: Identify Talent Pool (5-Stage Filtering)

Đề cử & Ứng tuyển & Phân bổ xem xét

Input: 9-Box (latest). Groups → Green Zone priority; Center Blue Zone optional.

Provide written rationale; avoid discriminatory justifications.

  • Basic Info
  • Succession Preferences (≥1 same dept; cross-dept if relevant)
  • Reasons per choice
  • Role-specific: Managerial (leadership + motivate example) / Non-managerial (technical + SBI); optional self-leadership
  • Mobility & Availability
  • Career Aspiration & Motivation (1–3y)
  • Lists: Internal · Incoming · Outgoing
  • Assess alignment; view cross-dept moves as development opportunities
  • Endorsed candidates → Step 3
  • Not selected → Thank-you note + encouragement

STEP 3: Gap Assessment

Đánh giá khoảng cách năng lực

Standalone mirror module; self-assessment, manager validation, role-based access; mock-data UAT.

  • System_Module_Feature_List.xlsx

STEP 4: IDP Execution – Closing the Gap

Thực thi IDP – thu hẹp khoảng cách

AHR workshop → Successor drafts IDP → Approvals (Leader, HoD) → Quarterly updates.

  • 70 Doing – projects, acting role, rotation
  • 20 Others – coaching, peer groups, mentoring
  • 10 Formal – courses, certifications

Quarterly updates; leader audit; notify changes (aspiration/strategy). Power BI portfolio view.

STEP 5: Talent Review Presentation

Báo cáo tiến độ & phản biện hội đồng

Annual (suggest Aug). Successor presents IDP progress → panel feedback → revise IDP → update dashboard.

  • Priority learning opportunities
  • Cross-functional visibility
  • Strategic projects participation
  • Eligible for Asia Domination (General Staff)

STEP 6: Continuous Review & Yearly Refresh

Theo dõi liên tục & làm mới hằng năm

Semi-Annual

  • % critical roles with ≥1 successor
  • % on-time IDP updates
  • % IDP actions completed
  • Participation rate (bootcamps, coaching…)

Annual

  • Internal fill rate for critical roles
  • % successors advancing readiness
  • Talent Pool retention (12–18m)

Collect successor & HoD feedback; evaluate training effectiveness; refresh pool post Y/E; communicate updates; tune tools & process.
